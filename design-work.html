Lemon Twist

Made in photoshop, one day I was sketching somethings in my notebook and I combined an L and a T together and it formed into something I liked. I came up with a name that had both the L and T together and Lemon Twist is what came to my mind. I then gathered inspiration after shopping at the grocery store and picking up a lemon the background came to my mind, followed by fonts to use. I had the idea of mixing the L and T as a handwritten font then the the rest as a sans serif font.

Made with:

  • Adobe Photoshop
