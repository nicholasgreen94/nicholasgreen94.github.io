About

My name is Nicholas Green. I am a self-taught web designer/developer that is currently living in Phoenix, Arizona. Originally from Salt Lake City, Utah. However at the age of 21 seeking new scenery, adventure, and climate, moved down to the valley of the sun. My interest in web design and development happened two years ago when I found a book at Barnes & Noble on the subject. I read the book. After that, became consumed by the matter, learning from various resources when I came home from my full-time job. My main resource that I learned from was Team Treehouse, learning from their videos, quizzes, and exercises. I have a passion for creating websites and designing them for all sorts of devices.

When not working or learning, I read books, write, drink coffee at new coffee shops, take pictures, learn Spanish, explore the nature of Arizona and spending quality time with my wife Valerie. I am inspired by nature, music(specifically folk, jazz and indie), design, photography, books, space and technology.

The following subjects I have studied:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • SASS
  • JavaScript
  • jQuery
  • Bootstrap (3 & 4)
  • ZURB Foundation
  • Responsive Web Design
  • Github
