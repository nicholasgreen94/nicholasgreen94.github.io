Coffee Lab

Coffee

Americano

Our Americanos are individually made to your preferences. Typically we use shot of espresso (30 ml) with twice the amount of water (60 ml).

Cappuccino

One of the most popular espresso drinks, a cappuccino done right should be equal parts espresso, steamed milk and foamed milk (about 60 ml each).

Mocha

With 60 ml of espresso, 60 ml of chocolate and 30 ml of steamed milk, a mocha is a the right choice if you want something sweet.

Latte

The latte gets its name because it's full of milk. It is generally just a 60 ml shot of espresso with 300 ml steamed milk -- and only 2ml foamed milk.

Tea & Pastries

Green Tea

Green tea is one of nature's most potent antioxidants and is gaining widespread popularity due to its natural ability to promote weight-loss and good health.

Black Tea

Black teas are especially rich in theaflavins and thearubigens (potent antioxidants) which have shown impressive cholesterol-lowering abilities and cardiovascular benefits.

White & Fruit Tea

Due to its unique processing and season of harvest, white tea is reported to have the highest antioxidant content (even higher than green tea!) and lowest caffeine level of all true teas. White tea also contains stress-relieving, mood-lifting properties.
